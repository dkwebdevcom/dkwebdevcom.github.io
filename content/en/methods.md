## Drain Cleaning Methods

Every service requires an individual approach. That's why we use different methods, adapting them to the specific problem.

---

## Mechanical Cleaning

### How Does It Work?

Mechanical cleaning uses special equipment to physically remove blockages from pipes. Our professional **Rothenberger R600** machine allows for precise work in pipes of various diameters.

### For Which Problems?

Mechanical cleaning is ideal for:
- **Hair and food residue** - the most common problem in bathrooms
- **Sand and sediment** - accumulated deposits
- **Construction blockages** - remnants from work
- **Medium-level blockages**

### Advantages

✅ Quick results  
✅ Effective for most blockages  
✅ Safe for pipes  
✅ More affordable than hydro-dynamic methods  
✅ Available 24/7  

---

## Hydro-Dynamic Cleaning (WUKO)

### How Does It Work?

Hydro-dynamic cleaning uses **water under high pressure** to remove blockages. This method is more advanced and effective for difficult cases.

Water at 150-200 bar pressure:
- Breaks down fats
- Removes limescale buildup
- Cleans pipe walls
- Does not damage installations

### For Which Problems?

Ideal for:
- **Grease blockages** - very difficult to remove
- **Recurring blockages** - problems that come back
- **Accumulated sediment** - long-term contamination
- **Preventive cleaning** - regular system maintenance

### Advantages

✅ Most effective method  
✅ Removes even difficult blockages  
✅ Cleans the entire pipe  
✅ Prevents recurring blockages  
✅ Ideal for restaurants and businesses
