## About Sewage Emergency Wrocław

We are a team of experienced specialists in the field of drain unclogging and sewage system cleaning. With years of experience in the industry, we have earned the trust of hundreds of clients in Wrocław and surrounding areas.

### Our Values

- **Professionalism** - Highly qualified technicians with extensive experience
- **Speed** - Arrival within 30-60 minutes, 24/7 availability
- **Quality** - Modern equipment, service guarantee
- **Honesty** - Transparent pricing without hidden costs
- **Reliability** - Over 10 years of market presence

### Our Equipment

We have modern equipment at our disposal:

- **Rothenberger R600** - professional drain cleaning machine
- High-pressure WUKO pumps
- Mechanical cleaning equipment
- Inspection camera for diagnostics

### Service Area

We operate in **Wrocław and surrounding areas within a radius of up to 20 km**.

### Community Commitment

We believe in building long-term relationships with our clients. Our goal is not only to solve the problem, but to give you peace of mind and confidence that your sewage system is in good hands.
