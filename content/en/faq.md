## Frequently Asked Questions

### 🔧 What services do you offer?

We offer professional **drain unclogging and sewage system cleaning**, blockage removal in pipes, sinks, washbasins, toilets, showers and drain stacks. We serve private clients and companies.

### 🚿 What types of blockages do you remove?

We remove all types of blockages:
- Grease blockages
- Hair and food residue
- Sand and sediment
- Construction blockages
- Difficult and recurring blockages

### 🛠 What methods do you use to clean drains?

We use two main methods:
- **Mechanical cleaning** - effective for most blockages
- **Hydro dynamic cleaning (WUKO)** - high-pressure water for difficult cases

We select the appropriate method individually for each type of problem.

### ❓ Do I need to break walls or floors?

In **most cases no** — we work non-invasively. In rare situations requiring access to pipes, we always consult with the client before starting work.

### 🏠 Do you service apartments and single-family homes?

**Yes**, we perform drain unclogging in:
- Apartments
- Single-family homes
- Restaurants
- Offices
- Industrial facilities

### ⏱ How quickly can you arrive?

In emergency cases, we offer **fast arrival within 30–60 minutes**. In standard situations, we arrive the same day or the next day.

### 🌙 Do you work 24/7?

**Yes**, we are available around the clock — 24/7, also on **weekends and holidays** with no extra charges.
