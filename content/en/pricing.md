---

## How We Determine the Price?

Price depends on:

1. **Type of blockage**
   - Simple blockage (hair, food residue) - lower cost
   - Advanced blockage (grease, scale) - higher cost

2. **Difficulty level**
   - Easy access - lower cost
   - Difficult access - higher cost

3. **Working time**
   - Typical 30-60 minutes - standard price
   - Advanced problem 2-3 hours - individual quote

4. **Working method**
   - Mechanical cleaning - cheaper
   - Hydro-dynamic cleaning - more expensive
