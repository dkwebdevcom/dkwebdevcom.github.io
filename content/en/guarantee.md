## Service Guarantee

We are confident in the quality of our work. That's why we provide a **guarantee on all services performed**.

---

## How Long Is the Guarantee?

### Drain Unclogging

- **6 months warranty** on drain unclogging work
- In case of blockage in the same place — we **unclog for free**

### Hydro-Dynamic Cleaning (WUKO)

- **12 months warranty** on cleaning work
- If the problem returns during the warranty period — **free repeat service**

---

## Warranty Conditions

The warranty applies if:

✅ **Proper operation** of the installation is maintained  
✅ **No pollution** of the sewage system (e.g., not throwing paper towels)  
✅ Following **recommendations of our technicians**  
✅ No **improper repairs** done by other people  
✅ No **physical damage** to pipes after our work

---

## What Does the Warranty Cover?

### Covers:

- Re-removal of the same blockage during the warranty period
- Consultation in case of problems
- Travel and diagnostics at no extra charge

### Does Not Cover:

- Pipe damage due to age or poor condition
- Blockage caused by sewage pollution
- Problems resulting from lack of proper maintenance
- Services outside our service area
