## How to Describe Your Problem?

To help you faster, tell us:

1. **Where is the problem?**
   - In the sink, washbasin, toilet, shower, or drain stack?

2. **How does it manifest?**
   - Is water not draining, unpleasant smell, or air bubbles?

3. **How long has it been happening?**
   - Is it a new problem or recurring?

4. **Is it an emergency?**
   - Can it wait, or do you need immediate help?
