## Emergency Signs

### ⚠️ Is It an Emergency?

Call immediately if:

- 🚨 **Water is not draining** from any source
- 💧 **Water is backing up** in the washbasin, sink or toilet
- 😖 **Unpleasant smell** from the sewage
- 🫧 **Air bubbles** when water drains
- 🏠 **Water is leaking** from walls or floor
- 🔴 **Multiple blockages** in a short time
- 🌊 **Water is coming up** from the bathroom floor
- 🪠 **Normal unclogging didn't help**
