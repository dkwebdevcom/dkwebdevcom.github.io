## Our Services

We offer **professional drain unclogging and sewage system cleaning** for private clients, companies, restaurants, offices and industrial facilities.

### Main Services

#### 🔧 Drain Unclogging
Fast and effective removal of blockages in pipes, sinks, washbasins, toilets and showers. We work non-invasively - in most cases there is no need to break walls or floors.

#### 💧 Drain Cleaning
Professional cleaning of the entire sewage system. We remove deposits, scale and accumulated contaminants.

#### 🚿 Removal of Various Types of Blockages
- Grease blockages
- Hair and food residue
- Sand and sediment
- Construction blockages
- Difficult and recurring blockages

### Work Methods

We select the appropriate method individually for each type of problem:

#### 🔨 Mechanical Cleaning
- Effective on most types of blockages
- Safe for pipes
- Quick results

#### 💨 Hydro Dynamic Cleaning (WUKO)
- High-pressure water
- Ideal for difficult blockages
- Thorough cleaning
- Does not damage installations
